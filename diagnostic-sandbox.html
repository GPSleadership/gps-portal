14-Day Executive Leadership Diagnostic

Jordan Carter · CEO, Carter Truck & Equipment Group
Alex D. Tremble, Founder & CEO · GPS Leadership Solutions · May 2026

Executive Summary

Top 3 Strengths

1. Decisive, action-oriented operator. Your team and peers consistently describe you as decisive and willing to make hard calls. When something breaks, you move fast and remove obstacles.

2. Customer-obsessed and on the floor. Leaders and frontline managers see you as deeply committed to customers. You show up in branches and will do whatever it takes to protect uptime and service.

3. High standards and strong work ethic. You're known as someone personally willing to outwork most people. That sets a strong tone for accountability and pride in the work.

Top 3 Development Priorities

1. Shift from chief problem-solver to builder of problem-solvers. Leaders still bring issues to you that their level should own.

2. Clarify and protect the few priorities that really matter. Too many "top priorities" and frequent changes in focus make it hard for leaders to know what to say no to.

3. Model calm, predictable communication under pressure. When you're frustrated, it shows in tone and volume — creating hesitation to bring bad news early.
