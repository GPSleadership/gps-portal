# Welcome Email Copy — Portal Access + Save to Device

Drop-in language for the client welcome email (and a short sponsor variant).
Voice: first person, direct, calm. No hype, no em dashes. Replace the bracketed
fields and the portal link with the recipient's real values.

---

## Client welcome email

**Subject:** Your GPS portal is ready, [FirstName]

Hi [FirstName],

Your private GPS Leadership portal is ready. It is where your 90-day plan,
weekly check-ins, and your results live, and it is also where you can reach me
between sessions through Ask Alex.

Open your portal:
[Open my portal]  →  [PERSONAL_PORTAL_LINK]

That link is yours alone, so please do not forward it. It signs you in
automatically.

**Save it so it is one tap away.** Most of my clients run their week from their
phone, so I would set this up once and forget it:

- On your iPhone: open the link in Safari, tap the Share button at the bottom,
  then choose "Add to Home Screen." You will get a GPS icon you tap like any app.
- On Android: open the link in Chrome and tap "Add to Home screen" when it
  offers, or use the menu in the top right.
- On your computer: bookmark the page (Cmd+D on Mac, Ctrl+D on Windows). In
  Chrome or Edge you can also click the install icon in the address bar to keep
  it in your dock.

The portal will also offer to do this for you the first time you open it, and on
a computer it will show a QR code so you can send it straight to your phone.

A good first step this week: open the portal, read your plan, and submit your
first check-in. That is the whole rhythm, and it takes about five minutes.

Talk soon,
Alex

Alex Tremble
GPS Leadership Solutions
