What the team is taking back to work

One theme ran through nearly every response: pause before you respond. Here is where leaders said they'll put it to work.

Pause before responding — buy a beat instead of reacting
Prep 1–3 likely tough questions before high-stakes meetings
Lead with "the one thing" — state the main point first
Clarify or punt with confidence when you don't have the answer

One action each leader committed to

Every participant left with a specific next step. A representative sample, in their words:

"A 10-minute prep before high-stakes meetings."
"Be intentional about pausing before speaking, and practice go-to clarifying questions and punt statements."
"Use 'the one thing' to highlight my main point at the top."
"Bring solutions, not problems."
One thing we'll build on next time. The most common request was to come in with a few pre-designed practice scenarios so leaders spend more of the hour practicing and less of it inventing situations on the spot. We'll fold that in — a small change that will make an already-strong session sharper.
